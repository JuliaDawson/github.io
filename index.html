Shared Prosperity around the World

Gini Index measures the deviation of prosperity (distribution of income or consumption expenditure) from a perfectly equal distribution, within a population.
A Gini index of zero represents perfect equality and 100 represents perfect inequality. However, in our data zero means that no Gini Index was available.
