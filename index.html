Shared Prosperity around the World

Gini Index measures the extent to which prosperity (distribution of income or consumption expenditure) within an economy deviates from a perfectly equal distribution.
A Gini index of zero represents perfect equality and 100 represents perfect inequality. However, in our data zero means there was no Gini Index was available.
